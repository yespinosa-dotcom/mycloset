What: I was searching for a pair of oversized vintage jeans during this time because I wanted pants that would give me a relaxed fit, and could also be styled to look masculine, androgynous or feminine. Super versatile. I found these in a bin and they were perfect. I was with my sister, and she was also really happy and she immediately was like, "lets share!" For a while I was wearing these a lot, but then I felt like they fit me weird, so I gave them to my sister. But then they were mine at one point, then hers, then mine again. It was both a competitive sibling ownership thing and also an accidental thing.

Memories: These remind me of my time at community college. I wore these a lot during that time. They went with everything.

I don't really like that theyre light wash because its not a color I really like to reach for, and I also feel like its a little too causal for my taste. They are super comfortable and thick so I just don't feel like giving them up yet. The pockets are huge which is awesome, and theyre perfectly long. Im back and forth on this every two years, I think twice now ive felt this and given them to my sister, but then asked for them back which makes her really mad.
